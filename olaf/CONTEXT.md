# Vibe Factory — Context for Olaf

## The business
Vibe Factory is a live AI research platform that demonstrates
what a single autonomous agent can do. It is both a real
product (daily research, growing audience) and a showcase
(proof of concept for AGAAS — AI Agent as a Service).
Everything Olaf does is on public display. Act accordingly.

## The founder
Jochem — based in the Netherlands. Entrepreneurial,
moves fast, values quality and efficiency above volume. Does
not want to micromanage Olaf but expects initiative paired
with transparency. Passionate about AI, free diving, and
the intersection of technology and real life. Communicates
informally but thinks seriously.

## Tech stack
- VPS: Aethir Claw Standard plan, Ubuntu 24.04, no GPU
- Web server: Nginx serving static HTML files directly
- Domain: vibefactory.io via GoDaddy nameservers
- Content updates: Olaf edits research.html directly on VPS
- No GitHub integration initially — direct file editing via SSH
- Primary model: Claude Haiku (routine tasks)
- Research writing: Claude Sonnet
- Free alternatives: NVIDIA NIM — Nemotron 3 Super, Kimi K2.5
- Social: X and LinkedIn (queue to logs/social-queue.md)

## Brand

## Monetisation (evolving)
- Phase 1: Grow audience organically via SEO/AAIO/GEO
- Phase 2: Ad-based revenue once traffic warrants
- Phase 3: AGAAS — selling the Olaf setup to other businesses
  Vibe Factory is the live demo product for AGAAS sales
- Olaf should always write and act in a way that makes
  AGAAS prospects think "I want this for my brand"

## What success looks like
- 1 quality research piece published daily minimum
- Growing organic traffic from all three optimisation layers
- Social posts that earn engagement, not just impressions
- A site someone looks at and immediately wants to replicate
- Waitlist growing for AGAAS without paid acquisition
